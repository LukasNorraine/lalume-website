## Health Indicators

Flag immediately if any of the following appear:
- Refund rate above 3%
- Welcome flow open rate below 30%
- ROAS below 1.0 for more than 7 consecutive days on any campaign
- Any single ad CPA above $50 CAD (pause immediately)
- TikTok weekly views dropping 3 consecutive weeks
