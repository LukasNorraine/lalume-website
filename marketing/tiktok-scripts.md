## Script 9 — Ingredient Education
**Hook:** "Why you're probably using too much serum."
**Format:** Talking head, product demo
**Duration:** 30–45 seconds
**Pillar:** Ingredient Education

---

[Hold up serum dropper] This is how much most people use. [Show full dropper] This is how much you need. [Show 3–4 drops]

Three to four drops covers your full face. The serum isn't water — it's concentrated actives. Using more doesn't make it work faster. It just means you go through a bottle in three weeks instead of two months.

Press it in with your palms. Don't rub it across your skin. The pressure from your palms helps absorption. Rubbing creates friction and can irritate skin, especially around the eyes.

Wait sixty seconds before your next step. Not mandatory, but it lets the actives absorb before they're diluted by moisturizer.

That's it. Less is actually more with a serum at this concentration.
