# LaLume Meta Ad Creative Briefs — Month 2 Launch

5 concepts for initial testing. Run in Advantage+ Shopping Campaign at $15/day.
Optimize for purchases from day 1. All copy in brand voice: factual, anti-hype, direct.

---

## Ad 1 — Ingredient List Static
**Format:** Static image
**Placement:** Feed + Stories
**Copy direction:** Radical transparency

**Visual:**
White background. Centered text listing the 5 active ingredients in the Niacinamide Serum, one per line, with concentrations:

```
Niacinamide — 10%
Zinc PCA
Sodium Hyaluronate (low mol. weight)
Panthenol (Vitamin B5)
Allantoin

15 ingredients total. Nothing you can't pronounce.
```

LaLume wordmark at the bottom in forest green.

**Headline:** "15 ingredients. Nothing you can't pronounce."
**Primary text:** "Most serums use 2% niacinamide and call it an active. We use 10% — because that's the dose with clinical evidence behind it. Every ingredient disclosed. No proprietary blends."
**CTA button:** Shop Now
**URL:** Niacinamide Serum product page

**Why it works:** Disrupts the feed with text-only visual. Speaks directly to the ingredient-aware buyer who reads labels. Zero production cost — Canva in 10 minutes.

## Ad 2 — Price Comparison Static
**Format:** Static image or simple two-column graphic
**Placement:** Feed
**Copy direction:** Category price comparison (never name competitor brands)

**Visual:**
Two-column layout. Left: "Niacinamide Serum. Category average: $65–$120." Right: "LaLume: $28." Below both columns: "Same clinical actives. One has a $40M marketing budget."

**Headline:** "Same actives. Very different reasons for the price."
**Primary text:** "High-end serums cost more because they spend more on campaigns, not formulas. The active ingredients in a serum cost roughly the same regardless of retail price. We put the budget in the bottle."
**CTA button:** See what's inside
**URL:** Niacinamide Serum product page (ingredient section anchor)

**Why it works:** Addresses the price objection before it's raised. Positions the low price as a feature, not a liability. No competitor named — just category framing.

## Ad 3 — Review Reader Video
**Format:** Video, 25–35 seconds
**Placement:** Feed + Reels
**Copy direction:** Authentic social proof, no music, no filters

**Visual brief:**
Talking head, plain background. Natural lighting. No makeup on presenter (or minimal). Hold up bottle when mentioning the product.

**Script:**
"I want to read you a real review because I think it's more useful than anything I could say. [reads Priya D. review from Toronto]. That's the timeline most people don't know going in. Two weeks of nothing, then week four it clicks. If you've been thinking about trying it, the 30-day guarantee means you can. Link in bio."

**On-screen text:** Quote text appears as subtitles
**Headline:** "Read 847 reviews before you buy"
**Primary text:** "The timeline is the thing most people don't know. 2 weeks: not much. Week 3–4: the change. The 30-day guarantee exists because we know it works — and want you to be able to verify it for yourself."
**CTA button:** Read Reviews
**URL:** Niacinamide Serum product page (reviews section)

**Why it works:** Video earns more reach. Reading a real review out loud is more credible than producing one. No music keeps attention on the message. Low production — one take.

## Ad 4 — "What We Don't Add" Carousel
**Format:** Carousel, 5 slides
**Placement:** Feed
**Copy direction:** Formulation philosophy, negative positioning

**Slide 1 (cover):** "What we don't put in the formula — and why."
**Slide 2:** "No fragrance. Fragrance is the #1 cause of contact dermatitis in skincare. It serves marketing, not skin."
**Slide 3:** "No mineral oil. Creates a surface film. Doesn't penetrate. Common in budget formulas because it's cheap."
**Slide 4:** "No parabens. Debated science — we use cleaner preservatives because there's no reason not to."
**Slide 5:** "No proprietary blends. Every ingredient and its concentration on the label. You know what you're paying for."

**Headline:** "5 things you won't find in LaLume"
**Primary text:** "We didn't set out to make the cleanest formula in skincare. We set out to make the most effective one. Leaving out the things that serve marketing instead of skin is a byproduct of that."
**CTA button:** See the full formula
**URL:** Niacinamide Serum product page

**Why it works:** Carousel format drives swipe-through engagement. Negative framing ("what we don't add") is more memorable than positive claims. Each slide makes one clean point.

## Ad 5 — 30-Day Guarantee Retargeting
**Format:** Static image
**Placement:** Feed (retargeting only — viewed product / added to cart / no purchase)
**Copy direction:** Remove final purchase anxiety

**Visual:**
Clean card design, forest green background, white text:

```
30-day money-back guarantee.
No return required.
No online form.
Just email us.
```

LaLume wordmark below.

**Headline:** "Still thinking? You have 30 days to decide."
**Primary text:** "Try the serum for 30 days. If your skin doesn't improve, email us with your order number. We'll refund you within 3–5 business days. You keep the product. There's nothing to return and no form to fill out."
**CTA button:** Try risk-free
**URL:** Niacinamide Serum product page

**Why it works:** Retargeting audience already knows the product. The only remaining friction is purchase anxiety. The guarantee removes it. Clean, factual copy — no urgency tactics.

## Audience Targeting

**Campaign 1 — Advantage+ Shopping ($15/day):**
Let Meta optimize. Provide catalog + all 5 creatives. Let it run 7–10 days before drawing conclusions.

**Campaign 2 — Manual CBO (Month 3+, once pixel has data):**
- Ad Set A: Interest — skincare, clean beauty, Canadian women 25–44
- Ad Set B: Interest — ingredient-focused (Skincare Addiction, beauty science content)
- Ad Set C: Lookalike 1% of purchasers (requires 100+ purchase events first)

**Retargeting (Month 3, 500+ site visitors):**
- Viewed product, no cart → Ads 1, 2, 3
- Added to cart, no purchase → Ad 5 (guarantee focus)
- Past purchasers 30–60 days → cross-sell creative (build separately)

**Budget reallocation rule:** If a creative hits CPA above $35 CAD after 5+ purchases, pause and reallocate to the top performer.
