Here's how to use it well, and what to expect when.

How to apply

1

Apply to slightly damp skin after cleansing. Not dripping wet — just the moisture that remains after patting dry.
2

Press in, don't rub. Three or four drops for the full face. Press with your palms rather than dragging fingers across your skin.
3

Wait 60 seconds before applying moisturizer or SPF. This isn't critical, but it improves absorption.

What to expect

Week 1–2 — Some people notice a subtle glow or slight skin smoothing. Many notice nothing yet. Both are normal. The niacinamide is working at a cellular level that takes time to surface.

Week 3–4 — Most people notice changes in texture and pore appearance. Some notice foundation applying differently. This is the phase most people say "oh, this is working."

Week 6–8 — Cumulative results continue. Sebum regulation, skin tone evening, and barrier strengthening improve with consistent use.
