The Centella Brightening Serum pairs two evidence-backed actives that most formulas keep separate. Madagascar Centella Asiatica (Cica) contains triterpenoids — compounds shown to reduce inflammatory markers, accelerate barrier repair, and calm reactive skin. Niacinamide at a clinical dose handles brightening, pore refinement, and sebum regulation.

The result is a serum that works on two fronts at once: calming the underlying inflammation that causes persistent redness, while visibly evening tone and texture over time. No irritants. No fragrance. No actives that punish sensitive skin.

Best for: Redness, reactive skin, uneven tone, post-blemish marks, sensitive or combination skin
Size: 55ml / 1.85 fl oz
Texture: Lightweight ampoule — absorbs in seconds
Visible results: Calmer skin within 1 week · Brighter tone in 3–4 weeks

Who it's NOT for

  • Anyone primarily targeting deep wrinkles and collagen loss — the Copper Peptide Serum targets collagen synthesis more directly
  • Anyone expecting results overnight — the barrier-repair mechanism takes 7–10 days to produce visible change
  • Anyone with a known allergy to Centella Asiatica (rare, but patch test first if unsure)
Madagascar Centella Asiatica (Cica)
The anchor ingredient. Centella contains asiaticoside, madecassoyl, and asiatic acid — triterpenoids that reduce inflammatory cytokines, stimulate collagen synthesis, and accelerate barrier repair. Madagascar-sourced Centella is higher in active compounds than generic alternatives.
Niacinamide
Vitamin B3 at a meaningful concentration. Inhibits melanin transfer to surface cells (brightening), reduces sebum production (pore refinement), and strengthens the lipid barrier. Works synergistically with Centella — one calms, the other corrects.
Sodium Hyaluronate
Low-molecular-weight hyaluronic acid that penetrates below the surface layer, delivering sustained hydration where it supports barrier function. Keeps the serum comfortable for dry and combination skin types.
Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5)
Converts to pantothenic acid in the skin, where it supports cell repair and moisture retention. Particularly effective alongside barrier-repairing actives — it accelerates the recovery process.
Allantoin
A gentle keratolytic and soothing agent. Promotes controlled cell turnover, softens skin texture, and reduces sensitivity to actives. Contributes to the gradual improvement in tone and radiance over consistent use.
1
Cleanse. Apply to clean, slightly damp skin. The moisture helps the ampoule spread evenly and aids absorption.
2
Apply. Dispense 2–3 drops and press gently into skin using your fingertips — no rubbing. Focus on areas of redness or uneven tone.
3
Wait. Allow 45–60 seconds to absorb fully. The formula is lightweight and absorbs quickly with no residue.
4
Layer. Follow with your moisturizer. If using in the morning, always finish with SPF — niacinamide increases photosensitivity slightly.
5
Frequency. Use morning and/or evening. Centella is non-irritating at any frequency — most people use it twice daily from day one and notice calmer skin within the first week.
