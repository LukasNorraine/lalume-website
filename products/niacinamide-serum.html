The Niacinamide Clarity Serum delivers niacinamide at a true clinical concentration — 10%. Most products on the market dose at 2–5%, which clinical literature consistently shows is sub-therapeutic for pore reduction and sebum control. We don't do that.

Zinc PCA works synergistically with niacinamide to further control sebum and reduce the appearance of pores. Panthenol (pro-vitamin B5) calms inflammation and supports repair. Low-molecular-weight sodium hyaluronate delivers deep hydration without clogging pores.

Best for: Oily skin, large pores, uneven tone, acne-prone skin, hyperpigmentation
Size: 30ml / 1 fl oz
Texture: Lightweight water-gel serum
Visible results: Typically 4–6 weeks of daily use

Who it's NOT for

  • Anyone with a confirmed niacin allergy (uncommon, but check before use)
  • Skin primarily seeking anti-aging and firmness — the Copper Peptide Serum targets collagen synthesis more directly
  • Anyone expecting results in under two weeks — the mechanism takes time to produce visible change

Clinical reference: Bissett et al. (2005). Niacinamide: A B vitamin that improves aging facial skin appearance. Dermatologic Surgery, 31(7 Pt 2), 860–865.

Niacinamide 10%
The star. At this clinical concentration, it visibly minimizes pores, regulates sebum, fades hyperpigmentation, and strengthens the barrier. Studies consistently show 10% outperforms lower concentrations.
Zinc PCA
Works synergistically with niacinamide to control excess oil production and further reduce pore visibility. Also has mild antibacterial properties that reduce breakout frequency.
Sodium Hyaluronate
Low-molecular-weight hyaluronic acid that penetrates deeper than standard HA, delivering sustained hydration without heaviness. Essential for barrier integrity.
Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5)
Calms redness and inflammation, accelerates skin repair, and increases moisture retention. Particularly beneficial for skin irritated by active ingredients.
Allantoin
A skin-conditioning agent that soothes, softens, and promotes cell turnover. Helps the serum feel comfortable even at high niacinamide concentrations.

Cleanse. Apply to clean, slightly damp skin. The moisture aids penetration and reduces any initial tingling.

Apply. Dispense 3–4 drops and press gently into skin. Focus on areas of concern: nose, forehead, cheeks. Avoid the immediate eye area.

Layer. Follow with moisturizer. For AM use, always finish with SPF — niacinamide makes skin more sensitive to UV.

Frequency. Use morning and/or evening. If you're new to niacinamide, start with once daily for the first week. Most people tolerate 10% well — mild flushing in the first few uses is normal and temporary.
