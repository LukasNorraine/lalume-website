The Radiance LED Therapy Mask uses photobiomodulation (PBM) — the application of specific light wavelengths to biological tissue to stimulate cellular processes. This is not a gimmick. It is a modality with over 40 years of peer-reviewed research across dermatology, wound healing, and oncology.

Red light at 630–700nm penetrates to the dermis where fibroblasts live. It increases ATP production in these cells, which directly stimulates collagen and elastin synthesis. Blue light at 415–445nm targets Propionibacterium acnes — the bacteria responsible for inflammatory acne — destroying it through photoexcitation without the systemic effects of antibiotics.

Frequency: 3–5 sessions per week for best results
Session length: 10 minutes
Visible results: Typically 4–6 weeks of consistent use

Who it's NOT for

  • Anyone with photosensitive conditions (lupus, porphyria, solar urticaria) — consult a dermatologist before use
  • People currently taking photosensitizing medications, including certain antibiotics (doxycycline), isotretinoin, or St. John's Wort
  • Individuals with epilepsy or seizure disorders — the flashing light cycle may be a trigger
  • Anyone who has had eye surgery in the past 4 weeks — wait for full clearance from your surgeon

Clinical reference: Avci P. et al. (2013). Low-level laser (light) therapy (LLLT) in skin: Stimulating, healing, restoring. Seminars in Cutaneous Medicine and Surgery, 32(1), 41–52.

Red Light (630–700nm)
Penetrates 2–3mm to the dermis. Stimulates mitochondrial activity in fibroblasts, increasing ATP production and triggering collagen and elastin synthesis. Supported by hundreds of double-blind clinical trials.
Blue Light (415–445nm)
Targets porphyrins within acne-causing P. acnes bacteria, creating singlet oxygen that destroys the bacteria without damaging surrounding skin cells. Also has anti-inflammatory effects.
Photobiomodulation (PBM)
The science underlying LED therapy. The Endre Mester discovery (1967) and subsequent decades of research establish that specific light wavelengths produce measurable biological responses at the cellular level. This is why clinic LED treatments exist — we're bringing the same technology home.
