The Gold Collagen Eye Patches target the specific challenges of under-eye skin: extreme thinness, low sebum production, constant movement, and disproportionate exposure to environmental stress. Standard moisturizers don't penetrate efficiently here — which is why a high-concentration, occlusive patch format works better.

24K colloidal gold is not decorative. It acts as a conductivity enhancer, creating a galvanic effect that increases the depth and speed at which actives penetrate the under-eye dermis. This is why the gold format outperforms standard eye creams for acute visible results.

Contents: 60 single-use patches (30 pairs)
Usage time: 20–30 minutes per application
Best for: Dark circles, puffiness, under-eye lines, dehydration

Who it's NOT for

  • Anyone who is pregnant or breastfeeding — the formula contains retinyl palmitate, which is not recommended during pregnancy
  • Daily long-term use — 2–3x per week is the effective cadence; daily use offers diminishing returns and can sensitize thin under-eye skin
  • People with a known collagen or latex sensitivity — patch-test on inner arm before first use

Clinical reference: Goverdhana et al. (2021). Efficacy of collagen peptide eye patches on periorbital wrinkles and skin hydration. Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology, 20(3), 824–830.
