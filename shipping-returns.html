30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Try any LaLume product for 30 days from the date it's delivered to you. If you're not satisfied — for any reason — we'll refund you in full. No questions asked, no return required.

Here's exactly how it works:

  • 1
    Try it for 30 days from delivery — not from the order date. We count from when it actually arrived at your door, because you deserve the full trial period.
  • 2
    Email us at Lalumecare@gmail.com with your order number. That's the entire process. No online forms, no return shipping label to print, no hold times.
  • 3
    Keep the product. We don't need it back. If it didn't work for your skin, it doesn't help us to get it back.
  • 4
    Refund within 3–5 business days back to your original payment method. No store credit unless you prefer it.

The guarantee covers the product price. Limit: one claim per customer per product. It applies whether you've used all of it or none of it — we want you to actually try the product, not just open it.

Why we offer this

A guarantee with no friction is only sustainable if the product consistently works. We offer this because we believe in the formula — the clinical concentrations, the ingredient choices, the transparency. If it doesn't work for you specifically, we don't deserve your money.

It also keeps us honest. Returns are rare when products do what they promise. That's the incentive we want.
